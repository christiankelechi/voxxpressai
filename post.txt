🚀 Introducing VOX XPRESS AI: Your Ultimate Multilingual Translation Workspace! 🌍✨

I’m excited to share a project I’ve been working on—a premium, all-in-one productivity suite designed to break language barriers seamlessly.

VOX XPRESS AI combines advanced speech recognition, instant AI translation, and a built-in persistent workspace to keep your workflow uninterrupted.

💡 Key Features:
🎙️ Voice & Text Translation: Speak or type in real-time. Instantly convert English into dozens of target languages (French, Spanish, Arabic, Japanese, and more!).
🔊 Listen Back (TTS): Hear the translation clearly to ensure pronunciation accuracy.
🌐 Embedded Persistent Workspace: Browse the web or access tools like WhatsApp directly inside the app—no need to switch tabs!
💾 Smart Persistence: Remembers your login states and sessions (goodbye to manual WhatsApp QR scans every time).
🎨 Premium Dark Mode UI: A sleek, modern, resizable interface built using PyQt6.
Built from the ground up to boost productivity for remote teams, creators, and global communicators.
